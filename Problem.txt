Create a Simple Library Management Application
You can create tables as per your understanding.
You have to complete the task in 10 Days.
You can develop in any language.

Business rules
1. A student can have only one Library Card
2. A Student can issue only two books on a Library Card
3. Issued books have 10 days validity. After that its becomes due and a report is required of due books
4. If book returned with 10 days - no fees. After 10 days it should calculate and show dues @ Rs5/day.
5. At the time of return, option for Lost/Damaged and book should not be issued again.
